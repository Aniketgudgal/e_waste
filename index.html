Our Process

How We Handle Your E-Waste

A transparent, documented process from collection to certified recycling.

01

Request & Assessment

Contact us with your requirements. Our team assesses the volume, type of e-waste, and provides a tailored quote.

02

Scheduled Collection

Our certified team arrives at your location with proper documentation. All items are inventoried and packed securely.

03

Data Destruction

All data-bearing devices undergo secure data sanitization or physical destruction as per your requirements.

04

Certified Processing

E-waste is dismantled, segregated, and processed at our government-authorized facilities with zero landfill policy.

05

Documentation

Receive comprehensive compliance certificates, destruction certificates, and sustainability impact reports.
