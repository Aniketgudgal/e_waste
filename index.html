Our Process

How We Handle Your E-Waste

A transparent, documented process from collection to certified recycling.

01

Request & Assessment

Contact us with your requirements. Our team assesses the volume, type of e-waste, and provides a tailored quote.

02

Scheduled Collection

Our certified team arrives at your location with proper documentation. All items are inventoried and packed securely.

03

Data Destruction

All data-bearing devices undergo secure data sanitization or physical destruction as per your requirements.

04

Certified Processing

E-waste is dismantled, segregated, and processed at our government-authorized facilities with zero landfill policy.

Documentation

Receive comprehensive compliance certificates, destruction certificates, and sustainability impact reports.

Yes! We offer free pickup services for bulk quantities (10+ items). For smaller quantities, a nominal pickup fee applies. We also have drop-off centers across major cities where you can bring your e-waste directly.

We are fully certified and compliant with all regulatory requirements including: CPCB Authorization, ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management), R2 Certification, OHSAS 18001, and state-level SPCB permits. All certifications are available for verification upon request.

Standard pickup is scheduled within 3-5 business days. Express pickup (within 24 hours) is available for an additional fee. The actual pickup process typically takes 30-60 minutes depending on the quantity of items.
